About Assam

Assam, located in the northeastern part of India, is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, rich culture, and aromatic tea gardens. From the mighty Brahmaputra River to the lush greenery, Assam is a paradise for travelers seeking natural beauty and tranquility.

Top Places to Visit

  • Kamakhya Temple: A sacred temple dedicated to Goddess Kamakhya.
  • Majuli: The largest river island in the world.
  • Kaziranga National Park: Home to the famous one-horned rhinoceros.
  • Sivasagar: A historical town with ancient Ahom monuments.

Culture of Assam

The culture of Assam is a blend of traditions, festivals, and art forms. Bihu, the main festival of Assam, showcases its vibrant dance and music. The traditional silk garments like Muga and Eri are world-famous.

Delicious Assamese Cuisine

Assamese cuisine is known for its simplicity and unique flavors. Some must-try dishes include:

  • Assamese Thali
  • Masor Tenga (Tangy Fish Curry)
  • Pitha (Rice Cakes)
  • Duck Meat Curry
